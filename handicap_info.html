Handicap Certification

Each club should have a certified Handicap person. Certification can be obtained by completing the online course, workshops and/or webinars. The online course cost is $35 and can take approximately 4 hours to complete, but it does not need to be done in one sitting. Registraton in the online course also includes a printed copy of the Handicap Manual.
