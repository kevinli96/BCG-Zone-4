BCGA Zone 4 Women

The Women’s Zone 4 came into being with the merger between the previous British Columbia Ladies Golf Association and the British Columbia Golf Association in October of 2004. Prior to that merger our Women’s Clubs were from the former District 2 and about half of the former District 5.

These Districts had a long and proud history in the development of women’s golf in much of the Lower Mainland The partnership with the former BCGA Association creates an even better opportunity for all aspects of golf to grow in the Lower Mainland.

Our Zone encompasses many areas including Vancouver, North & West Vancouver, Richmond, Delta, Whistler, Pemberton, Squamish and the Lower Sunshine Coast.

There is a rich history of Amateur, Senior & Junior Championships carried forward from the former Districts. The future looks bright and we are excited about working with the Men’s Zone as true partners in the New Zone 4.
