BCGA Zone 4 Women

Welcome to the BC Golf Zone 4 Women's website.

Zone 4 covers a very large area including 36 public and private golf courses and clubs within Vancouver, Burnaby, Richmond, Delta, North and West Vancouver, Squamish, Whistler, Pemberton, the lower Sunshine Coast, Bowen Island and Point Roberts.

Our site was developed to assist golf club members within Zone 4. It provides current information on tournaments, team play, and captain information as well as retains the rich history of past and current Amateur, Senior and Junior Championship winners. We welcome your ideas and suggestions for additional information and enhancement to your site.
